# SHATTERED

> *The colors were not rewards. They were the pieces of a self, scattered across a fractured physics.*

A 2D platformer built for **SOFTEC '26 Game Jam** — Theme: **Broken Physics**

---

## What is SHATTERED?

You start as a pure black character. Basic movement. Nothing else.

Each stage breaks a different law of physics. Gravity flips. Time rewinds you against your will. The world lies about what is safe and what will kill you. Survive a stage and you absorb its color — and its ability. The broken rule becomes your tool.

By Stage 6, you have three abilities and a world in total chaos. Every time you die, your controls are reshuffled. The ability might have been Gravity Flip. Now it's something else. You don't know until you press it.

**6 stages. 3 abilities. One choice that changes everything.**


---

## The Character

You begin as a pure black silhouette. No color. No definition.

Each orb you collect layers a new color onto you. By the end of Stage 6, when you collect the white orb, all five colors cascade across the character simultaneously. The ending is not a cutscene. It is what you look like.
